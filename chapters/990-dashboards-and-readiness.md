# 11. Dashboards & Exam Readiness [3-4 hours]

_Milestone M10 · Exam domains 3 & 4_

**Goal:**
- Publish **AI/BI dashboards** on a serverless SQL warehouse that answer the 8 business
  questions, then confirm you're exam-ready with a full practice exam.

## Mandatory Materials:

**Videos:**
- Databricks Academy — *Databricks SQL & AI/BI Dashboards*

**Reading:**
 - [Studybook M10 — Dashboards & Readiness](https://github.com/msg-CareerPaths/databricks-training/blob/main/docs/studybook/M10_dashboards_and_readiness.md) (includes a 10-question practice exam)
 - [Dashboards spec](https://github.com/msg-CareerPaths/databricks-training/blob/main/dashboards/README.md)
 - [AI/BI Dashboards docs](https://docs.databricks.com/en/dashboards/index.html)

## Insurance Lakehouse:
 > 1. Build the **6 dashboards** in `dashboards/README.md` on a serverless SQL warehouse, each
 >    tile sourced from a **gold** table.
 > 2. Add a **date** filter (joined to `dim_date`) and a **product_line** filter widget.
 > 3. (Optional) refresh the dashboard via the **dashboard task** in your Lakeflow Job.
 > 4. Take the **10-question practice exam** in the M10 studybook chapter and review every miss.
 >
 > **Acceptance:** all 8 business questions are answered by a dashboard tile; you pass the
 > practice exam confidently across all 7 exam domains.
